AboutUs!

Our Vision

At Advanced Consulting Services, our vision is to foster a world where every voice matters, and every social cause has the opportunity to create a meaningful impact. We envision a society where collaboration and empathy drive positive change, and where individuals and businesses unite to address pressing social issues and contribute to the betterment of communities worldwide.

Who We Are

Advanced Consulting Services is a dedicated team of professionals committed to leveraging technology for social good. With a passion for empowering individuals and businesses to make a difference, we strive to provide a digital platform that serves as a catalyst for meaningful social change and community engagement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive and dynamic online space that encourages active participation in social causes and fosters a sense of unity among diverse communities. We aim to facilitate connections and collaboration between passionate individuals, businesses, and community organizations, empowering them to amplify their impact and drive sustainable, positive transformations.

Our Values

Guided by our core values of empathy, collaboration, and social responsibility, we are dedicated to fostering an environment of inclusivity and respect. We believe in the power of collective action and the importance of creating a supportive community that values diversity, equity, and the promotion of social justice.

What We Do

At Advanced Consulting Services, we provide a comprehensive social awareness platform that enables users to create, participate in, and promote campaigns focused on various social causes. Our platform serves as a bridge between change-makers and small businesses, facilitating the sharing of ideas, resources, and support, to drive meaningful social impact and promote community development.

Join Our Community

Join us in our mission to make a difference. Become a part of our vibrant community of change-makers, businesses, and advocates dedicated to driving positive change and fostering meaningful connections. Together, let's work towards building a more equitable and compassionate world for all.

Frequency Asked Questions

How do I create a campaign?
To create campaigns, you have to first register an account with ACS. You will be able to create a campaign under the "Create Campaign" option from your profile name.

How do I cancel my donation?
All donations will only be processed after 24 hours as a precaution. Please contact us at acshelp@acs.org for further information regarding a refund.

How to delete account?
To delete an existing account, go to "Manage Account" and press delete account. Your account will be temporarily deleted for 30 days, and then permanently deleted.
